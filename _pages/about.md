我是孟旭，主要从事遗传编程、强化学习与动态调度优化研究。2024 年 7 月至 2026 年 3 月在新加坡 A*STAR SIMTech 担任 Scientist，同时于 2024 年 10 月至 2026 年 3 月在南洋理工大学（NTU）联合任职 Postdoctoral Research Fellow。

I am Meng Xu. My research focuses on genetic programming, reinforcement learning, and data-driven optimization for dynamic scheduling and decision making. I worked as a Scientist at A*STAR SIMTech (Jul 2024 - Mar 2026), with a joint appointment as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at NTU (Oct 2024 - Mar 2026).

-----------------------

# 研究方向 / Research Interests
* 进化计算与机器学习 / Evolutionary computation and machine learning
* 遗传编程与超启发式 / Genetic programming and hyper-heuristics
* 动态调度与组合优化 / Dynamic scheduling and combinatorial optimization
* 多目标优化 / Multi-objective optimization
* 面向决策支持的强化学习 / Reinforcement learning for decision support
* 面向制造与雾边协同系统的智能优化 / AI for manufacturing and fog/edge workflow systems
